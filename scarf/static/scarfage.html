TA:CO3
Created by TA:CO for supporters living in the Puget Sound. Distributed on derby day against the Seattle Sounders Aug 30th at Century Link. The tuxedo on the front is a tribute to Fancy TA:CO, a tradition of dressing up in full regalia for Timbers matches in Seattle. The "Eyes in the Dark" slogan was contributed by Brent Diskin. Designwork by Mikal Bruken.

TA:CO
Timbers Army : Covert Ops is a group of Portland Timbers supporters living in the Puget Sound area. They live in Seattle, home of their rivals the Seattle Sounders. Their mottos are "Behind Enemy Lines" and "Eyes in the Dark". Established in 2013. TA:CO has a culture where scarfs are only passed in person.
