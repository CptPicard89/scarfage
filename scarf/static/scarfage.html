TA:CO3 Created by TA:CO for supporters living in the Puget Sound. Distributed on derby day against the Seattle Sounders Aug 30th at Century Link. The tuxedo on the front is a tribute to Fancy TA:CO, a tradition of dressing up in full regalia for Timbers matches in Seattle. The "Eyes in the Dark" slogan was contributed by Brent Diskin. TA:CO

 Timbers Army : Covert Ops is a group of Portland Timbers supporters living in the Puget Sound area. They live in Seattle, home of their rivals the Seattle Sounders. Their mottos are "Behind Enemy Lines" and "Eyes in the Dark". Established in 2013. TA:CO has a culture where scarfs are only passed in person.

Regional Groups The Timbers Army has several regional groups with their own cultures. Many of the regional groups have multiple versions of their scarfs, one for each year. Some of the original scarfs from their first years are highly sought after.
